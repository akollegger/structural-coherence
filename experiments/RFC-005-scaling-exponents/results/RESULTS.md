## RFC-001 §8.3 Prediction (for reference)

> The empirical growth order of $\epsilon(V)$ will correspond to $\delta$ substantially
> below 1 in domains with strong entity or schema clustering, and structure that aligns
> with the corpus's cluster topology will dampen more efficiently than structure that does
> not. $\delta$ is not constant: as a corpus densifies with growth, cross-cluster linkage
> rises and the measured $\delta$ drifts upward, so a domain's favorable early-life
> clustering may erode at scale. $\gamma$ should likewise be estimated rather than assumed.
